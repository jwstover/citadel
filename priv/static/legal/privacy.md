# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 27, 2024

## Introduction

Citadel ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our task management and AI chat application ("the Service").

By using Citadel, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google OAuth, we receive:

| Data | Source | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| Email address | Google OAuth | Account identification, communications |
| Name | Google OAuth | Display name in the app |
| Profile picture | Google OAuth | Avatar display |

### 1.2 User-Generated Content

Content you create while using the Service:

- **Tasks** - Title, description, priority, due dates, and state
- **Chat conversations** - Messages you send and AI responses
- **Workspaces** - Names, settings, and member lists
- **Invitations** - Email addresses of people you invite

### 1.3 Usage Data

Information about how you interact with the Service:

- Credit consumption and usage patterns
- Features accessed
- Login timestamps and session duration
- Actions taken (creating tasks, sending messages, etc.)

### 1.4 Technical Data

Automatically collected technical information:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device type and operating system
- Referring URLs
- Error logs and crash reports

### 1.5 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies for:

- **Essential cookies** - Session authentication, security
- **Functional cookies** - User preferences, settings

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

## 4. Data Retention

### 4.1 Active Accounts

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Service.

### 4.2 Deleted Accounts

Upon account deletion:

- Personal data is deleted within 30 days

Certain data may be retained longer if required by law (e.g., payment records for tax purposes).
